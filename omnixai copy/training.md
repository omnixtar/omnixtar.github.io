## **LLASMA Supervisor Certification Program**

Here's a realistic **cost estimation** for the **LLASMA Supervisor Certification Program** (10-week intensive training for 8–12 supervisors per cohort), based on 2026 market rates.

### 1. Trainer Headcount Recommendation

| Role                          | Headcount | Rationale |
|-------------------------------|-----------|---------|
| **Lead Trainer / Principal Architect** | 1 (full-time during bootcamp) | Designs curriculum, teaches core LLASMA + Phoscript modules, oversees capstone |
| **Supporting Trainers / Tech Leads** | 2–3 (part-time or contract) | Specialize in Phoscript Phase 2, Safety Layer, FFI, I2P/Omnihash, and red-teaming |
| **Total Trainers**            | **3–4**   | Best balance: 1 lead + 2–3 specialists |

- **Why only 3–4?**  
  The program is highly specialized and elite. Too many trainers dilute quality and increase coordination overhead. The Lead Trainer should be a true expert (ideally someone who has already worked on LLASMA or similar low-level AI infrastructure).

### 2. Trainer Fees (Cost to You)

Assuming US / global remote rates in 2026:

- **Lead Trainer** (Principal-level AI Systems Engineer):  
  **$350 – $500 per hour** or **$18,000 – $25,000 per week** (full-time equivalent during the 4-week intensive bootcamp).

- **Supporting Trainers** (Senior/Staff level):  
  **$250 – $400 per hour** or **$12,000 – $18,000 per week**.

**Total Trainer Cost per 10-week Cohort (realistic range):**

| Scenario                  | Total Trainer Cost per Cohort |
|---------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Conservative**          | **$180,000 – $240,000**      |
| **Mid-range (Recommended)** | **$220,000 – $280,000**      |
| **Premium**               | **$300,000+**                |

**Breakdown (Mid-range):**
- Lead Trainer: $22,000/week × 8 weeks effective = $176,000

- **Total ≈ $250,000 – $280,000** per cohort (including preparation and capstone mentoring)

### 3. Trainee Fees & Subsidy Model

Since these are future **employees/supervisors**, a **subsidy model** makes the most sense.

#### Recommended Pricing Structure

| Model                          | Fee per Trainee | Subsidy Level | Notes |
|--------------------------------|-----------------|---------------|-------|
| **Full Market Rate**           | $35,000 – $50,000 | 0%           | If treating as external executive education |
| **Heavy Employee Subsidy** (Recommended) | **$8,000 – $15,000** (payable) | **70–80% subsidy** | Trainee pays small portion; company covers the rest |
| **Full Sponsorship (Best)**    | **$0 – $5,000** | **90–100% subsidy** | Common for critical internal leadership programs |

**My strong recommendation:**

- **Trainee contribution:** **$10,000 – $12,000** per person (symbolic commitment)
- **Company subsidy:** **$25,000 – $35,000** per trainee
- **Total cost per trainee to the organization:** **≈ $35,000 – $45,000** (including trainer allocation, materials, compute, venue, etc.)

For a cohort of **10 trainees**:
- Total program cost (trainers + overhead): **≈ $320,000 – $380,000**
- Company net cost after trainee contributions: **≈ $250,000 – $300,000**
