The 200 locomotives act as motherships for **Wasp-Class Security Drones**.

* **Orbital Exclusion:** In 2026, the "Brahmin-America" and Indian coalition have established an international **Space Traffic Management (STM)** zone. Any satellite whose orbit crosses the elevator’s path is forced to de-orbit or move at its own expense.
* **Electronic Shielding:** Ground stations use powerful jammers to create a "Black Hole" of GPS and radio signals around the base of the elevator, making it impossible for a remote-controlled drone to navigate toward the anchor.
