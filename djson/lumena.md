## **DJSON: The Backbone of MindMesh**

### What is DJSON?

**DJSON (Decentralized JSON)** is a standardized, schema-less data format built for decentralized applications. It‚Äôs a secure, extensible evolution of JSON ‚Äî designed specifically for storing structured knowledge in a decentralized, peer-to-peer environment.

### Features of DJSON in MindMesh:
- **Schema Flexibility**: Store anything ‚Äî notes, bookmarks, images, audio, video ‚Äî all in a unified format.
- **Immutable & Versioned**: Every change creates a new version, so you can track evolution over time.
- **Cryptographically Signed**: Each DJSON object is signed, ensuring authenticity and integrity.
- **Interoperable**: Works seamlessly with decentralized storage protocols and blockchain ecosystems.
