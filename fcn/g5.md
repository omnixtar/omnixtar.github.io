**Phase 3: 2028 (Maturity & Competition) — $100–500+ million**

### Major Cost Drivers for Acceleration
- **Incentives/Liquidity**: Largest line item. Paying early suppliers and maintaining GRATIS value is critical for flywheel effects (similar to early crypto mining rewards).
- **Engineering Talent**: High-quality teams for decentralized systems, AI integration, and security (~$200–400k per senior engineer/year).
- **User Acquisition**: Marketing and easy onboarding tools to reach non-technical users.
- **Infrastructure & Audits**: Seed hardware, bandwidth support, and repeated security reviews.

### Funding Sources & Realism
- **Venture Capital / Crypto Funds**: Primary path (similar to Render Network, Akash, or early Filecoin raises).
- **Grants & Ecosystem Funds**: AI/open-source foundations, privacy-focused orgs.
- **Token Sale / Treasury**: GRATIS allocation for development (careful governance needed).
- **Revenue**: Early fees from premium features or enterprise usage.
