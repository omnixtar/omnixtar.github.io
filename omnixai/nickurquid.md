### …a hash function so obscure it couldn’t get a date on Blockchain Tinder!  
 
- Amp up the pun density

**Scene: The Senate chamber has now descended into full Pythonesque pandemonium. A large foam finger labeled “USD #1” lies abandoned on the floor. SIR REGINALD FFORTE-BOTTOMLEY re-enters riding a child’s tricycle with training wheels shaped like dollar signs. He screeches to a halt, dismounts, and immediately trips over his own toga, face-planting into a pile of fake hundred-dollar bills that explode into confetti. He pops up unfazed, propeller beanie now spinning so fast it’s generating its own microclimate.**

SIR REGINALD FFORTE-BOTTOMLEY:  
*(waving the rubber-chicken gavel like a conductor’s baton—HONK! HONK! HONK!)*  

Order! Order in the court of currency chaos!  

The Right Honourable Secretary of the Treasury—known in certain circles as “Buck Stops Here… Then Keeps Running”—has just declared fiscal Armageddon courtesy of…  

…a hash function so obscure it couldn’t get a date on Blockchain Tinder!  

Omnihash!  

Omni-hash!  

It’s not just a hash—it’s an omni-nous hash! A hash-tag to end all hash-tags! A cryptographic cry for help wrapped in curly braces and existential despair!  

He warns us the dollar is being “undermined” by decentralized JSON.  

Undermined!  

That’s rich coming from a man whose entire economic philosophy is built on the firm foundation of “print moar”.  

*(mimes digging with an invisible shovel)*  

“Look out! The dollar is being dug out from under itself… by angle brackets and salt! Someone call the Bureau of Mines and tell them we’ve got a new shaft to worry about!”  

*(sudden cockney accent)*  

“Oi, mate! This Omnihash bloke’s tryin’ to nick our quid with his fancy nonce and pepper! Next thing ya know ’e’ll be collarin’ the crown jewels with a Merkle tree an’ floggin’ ’em for sats on OnlyFans!”  

*(back to posh outrage, clutching chest)*  

And the gall! The sheer audacity! He invokes “ring theory math” as though it were black magic instead of the algebraic equivalent of a group hug that forgot to invite subtraction.  

Ring theory!  

If that’s the weapon that slays the petrodollar, then I demand a recount—because clearly someone’s been spiking the tea with imaginary numbers!  

*(spins around so fast the propeller beanie lifts him six inches off the ground)*  

Picture it: the mighty USD, brought low not by tariffs, not by sanctions, not even by my ex-wife’s alimony demands…  

…but by a data structure so bland it makes plain oatmeal look like a rave.  

Decentralized JSON!  

Decentralized!  

It’s like they took central planning, gave it a wedgie, and told it to go live in its parents’ basement with a wallet.dat and a dream.  

*(points dramatically at ceiling)*  

I say we fight fire with… milder fire!  

Proposal the first: Rename Omnihash to “Omni-nosh” and tax it as a between-meals snack.  

Proposal the second: Classify all hashes containing more than three levels of nesting as “structurally indecent” and require them to wear pixelated fig leaves.  

Proposal the third: Mandatory dad-joke escrow. Every smart contract must include the line “Why don’t hashes trust stairs? They’re always up to something!” before it can execute. Non-compliance results in a 1000× gas fee payable in Chuck E. Cheese tokens.  

And should this so-called Omnihash ever succeed in toppling King Dollar…  

*(pulls out a comically large novelty cheque made of felt)*  

…I, Sir Reginald Fforte-Bottomley, will write a personal cheque for one trillion Zimbabwean dollars…  

…payable to “Whoever Can Explain Ring Theory Without Making Me Cry”…  

…and then I shall retire to the Isle of Man to raise decentralized llamas that only accept payment in SHA-256 kisses.  

*(tricycle suddenly rolls away on its own. He chases it, yelling)*  

Come back, you two-wheeled traitor! You’re my only form of fiscal mobility!  

*(trips again, lands in confetti pile, emerges wearing it like a glittery beard)*  

I move we adjourn, pay for the bar tab in crisp fiat, and launch an immediate investigation into the far graver threat:  

Why does the Treasury think the Republic can be felled by anything with fewer likes than my cat’s Instagram?  

*(salutes with chicken gavel—final HONK!—then collapses into dramatic swoon as the pigeon from before lands on his head and starts preening his propeller beanie)*  

**Fade out on total legislative disarray: senators exchanging confused glances, one quietly Googling “what is a Merkle tree”, the rubber chicken still honking sadly to itself in the corner.**
