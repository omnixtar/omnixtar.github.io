### Omnihash: Pure Hash-Based Ownership, No Blockchain Bloat
Omnihash is dead-simple cryptography: SHA-256 → base64 → embedded in **DJSON** (Decentralised JSON).  
One hash fingerprints any digital thing — your commit, a video upload, an AI inference output, a "like" action, even a whole user identity. Recompute the hash from the data → instant, zero-trust verification. No miners, no gas, no chain congestion. Just math.

The killer feature: **Omni*Hash Contracts**  
Attach a signed DJSON contract to any source code repo (or fork). It declares:

> “Read freely. But to deploy, modify, or run this commercially, attach your own Omni*Hash Contract linking your hash + an authorized Omni* Agent’s hash. Otherwise, you consent to maximum court penalties for damages.”

It’s not a smart contract — it’s a **declarative, hash-enforced license** backed by existing copyright law. Free reading (as always in open source), royalties/enforcement only on real-world commercial use. Verifiable by anyone, enforceable where it counts.
