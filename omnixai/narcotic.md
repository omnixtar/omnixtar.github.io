## Omnihash as Schedule I Narcotic

- Make it sillier with puns

**Scene: The same Senate hearing room, but now the lights flicker like they're powered by a potato battery. SIR REGINALD FFORTE-BOTTOMLEY leaps to his feet again, this time wearing a propeller beanie over his toga, clutching a comically oversized gavel shaped like a giant rubber chicken. He bangs it once; it squeaks "HONK!" instead of making a sound.**

SIR REGINALD FFORTE-BOTTOMLEY:  
*(in a voice that somehow manages to be both posh and cartoonishly nasal)*  

Ladies, gentlemen, and assorted bewildered interns—lend me your ears! Or at least one ear. The other can stay home and guard the fridge.  

Our esteemed Treasury Secretary—let’s call him Secretary Buckminster Full-of-It—just delivered what can only be described as the most hash-tag dramatic performance since Shakespeare tried to tweet in iambic pentameter.  

He claims the United States Dollar—our dear old greenback, the currency that once made pirates say “Arrr, that’s a fine bit o’ tender!”—is now under siege…  

…by a GitHub repo.  

Not a Death Star. Not a rogue AI named Skynet. Not even my accountant after tax season.  

A single webpage. omnixtar.github.io/contract.  

That’s right. The URL so boring it could put insomnia to sleep.  

*(dramatic pause, adjusts propeller beanie so it spins backwards)*  

He called it a “Trojan horse”! I say it’s more of a Trojan pony—small, pastel-coloured, and probably comes with glitter stickers and a side of existential dread.  

And the name! Omnihash!  

Omni-hash!  

It’s like they couldn’t decide whether to call it “Omni” like the shopping mall god, or “hash” like what happens when you sneeze while eating corned beef. So they just smooshed them together like a cryptocurrency love-child nobody asked for.  

*(suddenly switches to mock-German accent)*  

“Ve haff vays of making you monetize your JSON!”  

Nein, mein Freund. Ve haff vays of making you scroll past seventeen stars and wonder who hurt you.  

*(back to normal, waving arms like an over-caffeinated octopus)*  

He warned of “digital feudalism”! Feudalism! Where lords own the land and peasants pay in likes and retweets!  

Well if that’s feudalism, then I’m the Duke of Decentralized Doritos, and my vassals are required to crunch loudly during Zoom calls.  

And let us not forget the pièce de résistance:  

“Preserving data types through ring theory math.”  

Ring theory.  

*(leans in, stage whisper)*  

That’s not a threat to the dollar. That’s a threat to anyone who ever failed Algebra II and now has flashbacks whenever they see parentheses.  

Next they’ll say the dollar is menaced by commutative property! Or—horror of horrors—long division!  

*(clutches chest, staggers backward)*  

I can see it now: the petrodollar collapsing because someone proved 2 + 2 = fish using modular arithmetic.  

*(recovers, points accusingly at the invisible Secretary)*  

So I propose the following emergency measures:  

1. Immediately classify Omnihash as a Schedule I narcotic—because clearly it’s making people hallucinate global financial collapse.  

2. Deploy the elite Dollar Defense Squad—three accountants, a retired IRS agent named Carl, and a very angry Roomba programmed to eat USB drives.  

3. Mandate that all future blockchain projects must include at least one dad joke per whitepaper. Failure to pun sufficiently will result in community service… explaining ERC-20 tokens to your nan.  

And finally—  

*(pulls out a tiny plastic crown, places it on his head)*  

—if Omnihash ever actually dethrones the dollar, I shall abdicate my Senate seat, move to a yurt in Mongolia, and mine theoretical bitcoins using only hope and a hand-cranked abacus.  

Until then, I move we adjourn, order pizza paid for in perfectly good fiat currency, and perhaps investigate the real national emergency:  

Why does the Treasury Secretary think a README.md file deserves its own War on Terror briefing?  

*(bows so low the propeller beanie falls off and spins across the floor like a rogue Roomba. He chases it, yelling “Come back, my aerodynamic dignity!” as the chamber dissolves into polite chaos)*  

**Fade out on a single confused pigeon that somehow wandered into the Senate, tilting its head at the rubber-chicken gavel still squeaking faintly on the desk.**
