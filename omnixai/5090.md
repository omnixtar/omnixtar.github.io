## NVIDIA RTX 5090 Token Incomes Daily / Annual

To estimate the **USD value** of tokens consumed if an **RTX 5090** runs as an AI inference server for **12 hours per day**, we compare its local throughput to the equivalent cost on commercial AI services (e.g., OpenAI GPT series, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, etc.) in March 2026.

This is the "equivalent value" — how much you'd pay cloud providers for the same number of tokens your local RTX 5090 generates.

### Key Assumptions & Realistic Scenarios (March 2026 Benchmarks)
- **Runtime**: 12 hours/day = 720 minutes/day = 43,200 seconds/day.
- **Throughput (tokens per second, TPS)**: Varies by model size, quantization, batch/concurrency, and framework (vLLM, llama.cpp, etc.). RTX 5090 excels at high-throughput for small/mid models due to 1.79 TB/s bandwidth and 32GB GDDR7.
  - **Small models** (7B–8B class, e.g., Llama 3.1 8B FP16/Q4, Mistral 7B, Qwen2.5 7B): 3,500–5,841 TPS (high batch/concurrency for serving; peaks like 5,841 TPS on Qwen2.5-Coder-7B at batch 8).
  - **Mid models** (13B–32B quantized, e.g., Qwen 32B Q4): 1,100–1,900 TPS.
  - **Large models** (70B+ quantized, e.g., Llama 3.1/3.3 70B Q4/Q5): 50–80 TPS single-GPU (limited by 32GB VRAM; often needs heavy quantization or partial offload; dual 5090 setups reach ~80–100+ TPS but we're assuming single card).
- **Commercial pricing** (per 1M total tokens, approximate blended input/output; prices have dropped significantly by 2026):
  - Flagship tier (e.g., GPT-5/GPT-5.2, Claude 4.x Sonnet/Opus, Grok-4): $5–$20 per 1M tokens (input $1–$5, output $10–$15; blended ~$10–$15 assuming typical 1:2–1:4 input:output ratio).
  - Mid/high-value tier (e.g., GPT-4o / equivalents, Claude 3.5/4 Sonnet): $2–$10 per 1M.
  - Budget/efficient tier (e.g., GPT-4o-mini/o4-mini equivalents, Grok fast variants, open models via providers): $0.5–$2 per 1M (often $0.15–$0.60 input + $0.60–$2 output).
  - Average realistic equivalent: ~$2–$8 per 1M tokens for comparable quality to what a local RTX 5090 delivers (mid-range open models like Llama 3.x 8B–70B quantized).

We calculate **daily tokens generated** = TPS × 43,200 seconds (assuming continuous high-utilization inference; real-world may be 70–90% due to setup/load).

Then **daily USD equivalent** = (daily tokens / 1,000,000) × price per 1M.

Annual = daily × 365.

### Key Caveats
- These are **gross equivalent revenues/savings** — not net profit. Subtract electricity (~$0.10–$0.20/kWh; 575W TDP → ~$250–$500/year at 12h/day), hardware depreciation (~$2,000–$4,000 card cost over 2–3 years), cooling/maintenance, and downtime.
- Commercial APIs often charge differently for input vs. output; blended rates vary by use case (e.g., chatbots have more output).
- Local run uses open models (e.g., Llama 3.x, Qwen, Mistral) — quality may not match top closed models like GPT-5/Claude 4, but is very close for many tasks in 2026.
- High TPS requires optimized setups (vLLM, good batching, quantized models fitting in 32GB). Real utilization rarely hits 100%.
