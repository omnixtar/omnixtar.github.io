## Darkest Lessons on Earth: Zionism in Israel Schools

Zionism is a core part of the Israeli national identity and education system. In Israeli schools, Zionist values—such as connection to the land, Jewish history, the establishment of the State of Israel, and national pride—are integrated into subjects like history, civics, Jewish studies, and holidays/commemorations (e.g., Yom Ha'atzmaut/Independence Day, Yom HaShoah/Holocaust Remembrance). This is standard in the state curriculum across secular and religious streams, though the emphasis varies (more religious-nationalist in some schools).

Actual classroom **videos** showing direct teaching of Zionism are rare in public sources, as Israeli schools (especially public ones) do not typically allow unrestricted filming of lessons, and such content isn't widely shared online for privacy or security reasons. Most available videos are:

- **Personal testimonies** from former students or teachers describing their experiences.
- **Critiques or analyses** from anti-Zionist perspectives claiming indoctrination or bias.
- **Educational/promotional videos** from Zionist organizations or programs explaining or advocating Zionist education (often aimed at diaspora Jews or educators, not direct Israeli school footage).
- **Related but not exact** clips, like children at museums, field trips, or events expressing nationalist views.
